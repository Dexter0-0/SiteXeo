Team registration video

One of the most intriguing and complicated tasks of our registration requirements was the video, in which we had to shortly present our team's hopes, spirit, and morality. During our brainstorming session, we came up with a tumult of innovative ideas about this project, the best one being the use of our town's historical objectives. It shows that there shouldn't be a dichotomy between technological progress and historical monuments and lessons. We started filming in the citadel of Alba Iulia, with the necessary precautions regarding the pandemic. Our creative processes began as a group photoshoot, alongside our alumni and Xeo Academy, to portray the idea of unity in the past, present, and future in our self-made family. Afterward, we took out time expressing what this group means to us, from a place where we can be who we want to, to a space in which we can relax, then we swapped locations, showing the beautiful parts of this corner of the world.

Every heartfelt dialogue that we included about the meaning of this array, the people, the way it helps us improve, and the emotions it sparks into ourselves. The scenery helped us a lot, but we can't say the same about the weather that made it harder to film. We succeeded nonetheless, despite the obstacles and camera anxiety that most of us had.
