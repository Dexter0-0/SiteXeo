Blood donation

We started with the idea that health is always the priority. And when our community needs us, we act. We discovered through some team members that the blood donation department in Alba Iulia does not have stable resources and that donors are always needed. Right as we learned this, we mobilized our team and tried as much as possible to mobilize the community in Alba. So, we started a campaign called "#PutemSalvaVieti" on this subject among all the robotics teams. So that each of them would go to their city and donate blood, and we urged our friends and family to follow our gesture.

The nurses were excited to see how eager we were to donate, so we assured them that we would come back with even more people to donate. Since then we have decided to continue the action and help a little when we can.
