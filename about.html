About Xeo

We are a robotics team from Alba Iulia but we are also a family. Xeo was founded in 2016 at “Colegiul Național Horea Cloșca și Crișan” and was one of the first teams to participate in First Tech Challenge Romania, being attributed the number RO001. Since the begining, we did our best to improve both technical and nontechnical, managing to represent Romania 2 times at the World’s Championship in Detroit. This family has grown over the years and has come to be way more than just a robotics team and is now a foundation for local youth to explore STEM and be active in their community.
